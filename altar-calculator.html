Altar Calculator

Choose your current Altar level and enter your current mithril and sacred water. The calculator shows exactly what you need for the next level. Alliance construction reduction applies to both mithril and sacred water costs; estimates for sacred water use your Blessing Fountains and selected timezone (mithril is not passively generated here).

Quick Start

  1. Set your current Altar level and target level.
  2. Enter current Mithril and Sacred Water to calculate what is still needed.
  3. Set Blessing Fountain levels to estimate Sacred Water income and readiness time.
  4. Choose alliance RSS reduction and timezone for current time and upgrade-ready clock.
