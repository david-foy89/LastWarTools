Choose your current HQ level and enter your current iron, food, gold, and oil. The calculator shows the next HQ requirement buildings plus HQ resource cost and build duration through level 35.

Quick Start

  1. Set your current HQ level and target HQ level.
  2. Enter current Iron, Food, Gold, and Oil to see remaining requirements.
  3. Use the time fields to align the estimate with your in-game timing.
