Last War Tools

These calculators help you plan upgrades and research by showing what resources you still need, your expected hourly income, and the estimated time until you are ready for the next step.

What The Calculators Are For

  1. Track remaining upgrade costs from your current level to your next target.
  2. Apply reduction bonuses to reflect alliance tech or profession effects.
  3. Model workshop, mine, farm, or factory output to estimate time-to-ready.
  4. Use season-specific planners for buildings that need multiple resources.
  5. Use troop planners to map T10 and T11 research progress with clear priorities.
